BioHackathon Europe 2022

An EasyBuild/EESSI project has been accepted at the next European BioHackathon:

Make your own or favourite software available on your cluster with EasyBuild/EESSI (project #16)

You can already register and come hack with us on EasyBuild/EESSI, on site or virtually!

BioHackathon activities are driven by practical sessions where people gather, discuss, and implement ideas and projects during intensive and productive coding sessions.
(Bio)Hackathons are a good place to do networking, and develop new or extend projects.

The European BioHackathon will be near Paris 7-11 November 2022, and is organized by ELIXIR Europe.
