EasyBuild User Survey

For the last couple of years, we have conducted a user survey in the EasyBuild community in an attempt to collect (anonymous) feedback, and to get better insight into the community.

The most recent survey was conducted in Dec'21-Jan'22.

The survey results are presented during the annual "State of the Union" talk at the EasyBuild User Meeting:

  • 2020 (4th user survey): slides (PDF) - recorded talk (YouTube)

  • 2018 (2nd user survey): slides (PDF) - recorded talk (YouTube)
  • 2017 (1st user survey): slides (PDF) - recorded talk (YouTube)
