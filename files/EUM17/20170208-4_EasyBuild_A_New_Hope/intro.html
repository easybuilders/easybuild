Preparing for JURECA


After years of JUROPA operation, JSC is preparing for decommissioning the cluster and deploying a successor.

With an increasing set of software requirements, the user support group desperately needed new means to manage compilers, MPI runtimes, libraries and applications.

Alan Skywalker O'Cais introduced EasyBuild to JSC, where it was received with entusiasm by a small group of rebels members of the team. But the battle ahead was just starting and many features where still missing...
